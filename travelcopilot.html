TravelCoPilot — AI Travel Planner

Role: Product Lead (concept → PRD → prototype) • Timeline: 8 weeks • Artifacts: Deck + Figma prototypes.

Problem

Travel planning is fragmented: users spend hours coordinating routes, budgets, and preferences across multiple tools. TravelCoPilot aims to provide a single assistant to plan, optimise and book multi-stop trips using AI orchestration.

Target users

  • Frequent travelers who value time saving
  • Leisure planners who want curated itineraries
  • Budget-conscious travelers who require cost-aware plans

My contributions

  • Led product definition and roadmap, prioritized features using RICE.
  • Created user journeys, wireflows, and success metrics (time-to-plan, conversion, retention).
  • Defined technical orchestration for AI + 3rd-party APIs and documented Colab proof-of-concept.
  • Built prototype requirements and connected Figma links in the deck for stakeholder reviews.

Approach / Execution

  1. Discovery: interviews, pain-point mapping, JTBD capture.
  2. Define: MVP scope (AI itinerary generator, booking links, multi-stop optimisation).
  3. Prototype: low → high fidelity in Figma; PoC Colab to validate AI prompts.
  4. Metrics & guardrails: time-to-plan, % bookings, NPS; privacy & rate limit considerations for third-party APIs.

Key outcomes (expected / goal)

  • Goal: reduce planning time by 70% for multi-stop itineraries.
  • Goal: convert 3–5% of users to booking flows within 14 days.
  • Baseline and experiment plan included in deck to measure impact post-launch.
