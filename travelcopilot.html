Problem

Travel planning is fragmented: users spend hours coordinating routes, budgets, and preferences across multiple tools. TravelCoPilot provides a single assistant to plan, optimise and book multi-stop trips using AI orchestration.

Approach

  1. Discovery: interviews, pain mapping, JTBD.
  2. Definition: MVP scope and success metrics.
  3. Prototype: Figma & PoC Colab for AI prompts.
  4. Measure: time-to-plan, bookings, retention.
