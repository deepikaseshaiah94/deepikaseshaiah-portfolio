HireMate — GTM & Product Strategy

Role: Product strategist (GTM) • Timeline: 6–8 weeks • Artifact: graded project doc.

Overview

HireMate is an AI mock-interview product aimed at job-seekers, combining free practice flows with premium feedback and coaching. The doc provides pricing options, funnel design and a 30/60/90 GTM plan targeted at college graduates and early-career professionals.

My contributions

  • Built the GTM funnel: acquisition channels, activation milestones and conversion triggers.
  • Designed pricing tiers aligned with student / lateral-hire segments and retention plays.
  • Defined success metrics and a 6-month acquisition projection to validate unit economics.

Key metrics & goals

  • Initial 6-month target: 10k users with 3–5% paid conversion.
  • Activation metric: complete first mock-interview & receive feedback (target 40% activation).
  • Retention: weekly active users and repeat mock-interviews as core retention loops.
