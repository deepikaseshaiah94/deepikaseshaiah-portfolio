SubSentry — Subscription Management (PRD)

Role: Product Owner (PRD & UX flows) • Timeline: 4–6 weeks • Artifact: PRD and wireflows.

Problem

Users lose track of recurring payments, face surprise charges, and lack a simple dashboard to manage subscriptions across services. SubSentry provides a clear dashboard, reminders, and retention-centric flows to reduce churn and save users money.

My contributions

  • Authored PRD: core flows (dashboard, manual entry, reminders), success metrics and onboarding funnel.
  • Defined retention experiments and notification cadence to reduce subscription churn.
  • Designed quick-entry UX to reduce time-to-first-value and validated with simple usability checks.

Success metrics

  • Time-to-first-value (add first subscription) < 3 minutes (MVP goal).
  • Weekly active users and reminder-driven retention > 20% after 30 days (experiment goal).
